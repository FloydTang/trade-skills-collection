# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and constructive experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional
- Assume good intent while staying factual
- Give actionable, kind feedback
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Repeated bad-faith disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, commits, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Contact

For conduct concerns, open a private maintainer contact request through GitHub.
